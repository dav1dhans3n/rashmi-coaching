REGULATIONS:

Hypnosis and Hypnotherapy are legal, recognized, and Federally unregulated industries. However, Texas is one of very few states that adds any oversight. The Texas legislature decided that hypnotherapists without a degree in psychology or psychiatry must refer to themselves as hypnotists, and claim that their services are for “entertainment purposes.” Therefore, regardless of my certifications, I refer to myself as a Hypnotist on this website.
