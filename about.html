Why was it so hard just being me?

Being rebellious, brave, questioning complacency, wanting to have uncomfortable conversations, wanting to be the change I wanted to see in the world…these were some of the questions I contemplated in my youth in India and they continued to haunt me as I migrated to the US.

Altering the status quo in every phase of my life made me an outcast. Deeply wounded, I started questioning and blindly chasing the purpose and meaning of suffering, of discrimination, of love and the claim of the existence of “God”. This quest left me utterly lonely in a foreign country. Even though my inner world was crumbling what kept me afloat was that my outer world was flourishing in the IT industry.

Due to my innate nature of being trusting, naïve, true to myself and making heart led choices, I experienced intense personal losses which left me severely depressed in a foreign country with suicidal thoughts. It was during this time that I contemplated suicide. While I was experiencing these darkest moments of my life, I had a “spontaneous spiritual awakening” which left me in a state of unconditionally love & euphoria for months, ending my suffering.

My Awakening...

Due to the awakening experience and with the help of many modalities like, life coaching strategies by Tony Robbins & Chloe Maddanes, Akashic Records sessions by Linda Howe and QHHT sessions by Cannon Dolores, I was able to find light, which helped me to create a life that I desired and loved.

It’s through my experiences that I have learned to live a conscious life filled with love, compassion and clarity. Being deeply rooted in the truth of existence has also guided me through tribulations, such as the loss of loved ones, with grace and understanding.

I am grateful for the gift of creating sacred spaces where people feel safe to show their vulnerable side, appreciate being seen, heard and understood. This gift is a critical asset which allows me to assist and guide individuals as they journey the unknowns of their life with a lot more love, compassion, clarity and confidence.
